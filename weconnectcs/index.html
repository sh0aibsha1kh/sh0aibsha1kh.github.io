Connecting Communities and Empowering People

What We're All About

Our Mission

To embrace, engage, empower and integrate people and make our communities more vibrant.

Our Vision

To see that every person has an equal opportunity to participate, contribute and integrate smoothly into the multicultural mosaic of Canadian Society.

Our Objectives

To create & implement Community Integration and Empowerment Programs for smooth settlement of vulnerable and marginalized members of the community.
