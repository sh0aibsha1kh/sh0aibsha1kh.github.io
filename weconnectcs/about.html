Who We Are

Mission

To embrace, engage, empower and integrate people and make our communities more vibrant.

Vision

To see that every person has an equal opportunity to participate, contribute and integrate smoothly into the multicultural mosaic of Canadian Society.

Objective

To Create & implement Community Integration and Empowerment Programs for smooth settlement of vulnerable and marginalized members of the community. To serve the community with appropriate language specific and culturally sensitive services with the objective of helping people walk through the challenges faced as newcomers and a successful settlement into Canadian life. The organization aims to work at the grass root level in the community to maximize capacity-building among youth, women, seniors and general community. Our services include settlement, empowerment and integration programs, women networking, seniors wellbeing and recreational activities. To provide support counseling to women affected by partner abuse and help them to become self reliant. The programs aim to eradicate isolation of vulnerable members especially seniors and women and work towards realization of individual potential of the participants.
