RydeShare

Power to the Driver

About

Vision

RydeShare is a decentralized platform built on the Ethereum network. Our goal is to eliminate the middle man to increase profits for drivers and decrease costs for passengers. The RydeShare network will consist of accredited and certified drivers offering rides to passengers that request the service. Payments will be set by the customer and then accepted by a nearby driver. Overall the RydeShare network will be a decentralized means of travel built by the people for the people.

Technology

The technology behind RydeShare will consist of open source applications developed for Web, iOS and Android. Payments will be processed using RydeCoin. The application will allow passengers to place requests for rides with recommended amounts of RydeCoin based on travel distance. The passenger will also be required to place a pre-deposit amount of RydeCoin to cover driver inconvenience due to cancellations. Once requests have been placed and approved, nearby drivers will be alerted of the available passenger. The first driver to accept the request will be awarded the contract. To prevent drivers from defaulting on accepted passengers a small deposit or RydeCoin will be requested from the driver. This deposit will be returned upon the customer payout indicating completion of the transaction.
