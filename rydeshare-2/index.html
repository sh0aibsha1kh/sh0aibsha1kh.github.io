What is Ryde?

Ryde is a decentralized platform built on the Ethereum network. Our vision is to build a ride sharing network that eliminates the middleman and increases profits for drivers while decreasing costs for passengers. The Ryde network will consist of accredited and certified drivers offering rides to passengers that request the service. Payments will be set by the customer and then accepted by a nearby driver. The main goal of the Ryde network will be to become a decentralized means of travel built by the people, for the people.

Technology

Ryde will consist of open source applications developed for the Web, iOS and Android. Payments will be processed using RydeCoin (RYD). The application will allow passengers to place requests for rides with fees determined in terms of RydeCoin based on factors such as demand, availablilty, distance, time, etc. The passenger will be required to place a deposit amount of RydeCoin to cover driver inconvenience due to events such as cancellations. Once requests have been placed and approved, nearby drivers will be alerted of the available passenger. The first driver to accept the request will be awarded the contract. To prevent drivers from defaulting on accepted passengers a small deposit or RydeCoin will be requested from the driver. This deposit will be returned upon the completion of the transaction.

Coin Supply

We plan on releasing 900 Million RydeCoins (RYD) in total. These coins will be partitioned in the following ways:

  • 100 Million RYD (Ecosystem Reserve)
  • 100 Million RYD (Company Reserve)
  • 700 Million RYD (Sale Coins)

Phases

Phase 1: 210M RydeCoins (1 ETH = 2500 RYD)

Phase 2: 365M RydeCoins (1 ETH = 2000 RYD)

Phase 3: 125M RydeCoins (1 ETH = 1500 RYD)
